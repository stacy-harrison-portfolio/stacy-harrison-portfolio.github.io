About me

Hello there!

I’m Stacy — a casual frontend developer and a Graphic Design student at the Faculty of Visual Arts at UANL, in MTY, MX.

Right now, I’m getting the hang of HTML, CSS, and basic JavaScript, while gradually exploring responsive design, Git, and Bootstrap.

I love problem solving through a creative lens, and studying UI/UX naturally led me to wonder about the full development process. As a designer, two questions kept popping up in my head:
“How would a developer build this?” and “What should I keep in mind for my design to be implemented properly?”
So, what better way to learn than to actually start coding?

I’m excited to keep improving my skills through hands-on projects. This portfolio is a work in progress (like me), so feel free to look around and see what I’ve been building.
